How to make your site look professional

The Right Colors For The Job

Simply put, different colors convey different emotions and ideas. To have a professional website, you have to pick the right colors. You have to pick the colors that convey the emotions you want the users to feel. A lot of website use the color white and varying shades of gray. This is to convey an air of "clean and crisp". You would use these colors on a website for a business. If they were to use black colors with white text, it would convey very differently. For example, try the button below.

It swapped the background colors and inverted the text colors! But did you notice how it affected the feel of the page? To me, it makes the whole page feel different. The gray seems so simple and inoffensive, and the white text of the inversion feels harsh, distracting, and mildly annoying. The whole page justgets so much brighter that it is easy to see why I picked the first color scheme. That button serves as a demonstration to illustrate the power of colors of a page.
